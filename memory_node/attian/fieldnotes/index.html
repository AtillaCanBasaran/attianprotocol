SPF-0185 // SHORT POETRY FRAGMENT

Pale Face

Status: recovered / emotionally fogged

What a pale face. I mean it is actually not very pale... it is just... pale looking? When you say "your face looks pale, is everything aight?" kinda pale. This doesnt even make sense to you, right? That exactly why i stopped making some, when everything else just started making none to me at all.

You look unhappy, you dont look sad, you dont look depressed, you dont look particularly tired. Wait... tired actually. You look tired... and pale.

Admin comment: Strong observational spiral. The subject successfully turned "you look tired" into a diagnostic corridor with bad lighting.

MUSE margin: pale is not a color, it is a room forgetting the sun.

DLF-3645 // DIARY LOG FRAGMENT

Spirals

Status: recovered / focus artifact

Somebody once told me that drawing spirals make her much calmer. She draws spirals to cope with focusing issues. I was doing that too, long time ago. Before when I stopped thinking at all.

However here is a fact proven by me. Universe is a path that revisits the same directions repeatedly while never visiting the same position. A cycle of repeating patterns that never return to the same place.

  • Start at a single point
  • Expand forever
  • Wrap around the origin forever
  • Never close
  • Never simply climb one direction

Ha-ha! Spiral-head-ing! Spiral-heart-ing! Spiral-soul-ing! That is your friendly neighbourhood Spiral-Man! or Woman! Or People!

Universe itself is a 3-D spiral. "Yeey", i guess!

Admin comment: The subject has converted a coping doodle into cosmology. Irritating, but thematically consistent.

MUSE margin: spiral means leaving and returning and not returning and also maybe snacks.

SPF-0637 // SHORT POETRY FRAGMENT

Becoming

Status: recovered / semantic weather unstable

Stars are annoying, shining and disturbing high on skies. Moon is too loud, nobody can even feel alone. Sun is too bright, nobody can even look at it. Birds sing songs nobody listens to. Clouds are just confused blobs. Trees so still and rotten. Warm air is so exhausting to breathe in.

hahaaha, things just become what you call them, see?
